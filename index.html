I’m a machine learning researcher with a Ph.D. in Machine Learning applied to Computational Biology from the University of Navarra. During my doctoral journey, I explored the fascinating world of graph learning—including knowledge graphs and graph neural networks—as well as a variety of representation learning approaches such as variational autoencoders and causal representation frameworks. A big part of my work involved integrating and analyzing multi-omics data like bulk RNA-seq, single-cell RNA-seq (scRNA-seq), spatial transcriptomics, and Perturb-seq.

🏅 I’m really proud to have published in top-tier machine learning venues such as ICLR 2025 and Nature Machine Intelligence, and to have been selected as a Fulbright Excellence Fellow 🇺🇸 for a research stay at the Center for Data Science at New York University.

These days, my focus has shifted toward financial machine learning. I’m working on the design and implementation of ML models for MFT (medium-frequency trading) strategies in the world of cryptocurrency derivatives—blending real-time prediction, smart strategy design, and performance-driven modeling.

Outside of work, I love taking on Kaggle challenges and am proud to be a Kaggle Competitions Expert . I’m passionate about solving complex problems and enjoy collaborating across disciplines—whether it’s in biology, healthcare, or finance.
